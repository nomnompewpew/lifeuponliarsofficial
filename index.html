OUR STORY

Life Upon Liars was established in 2014 and re-vamped in 2022. Crossing styles of punk rock, metalcore and melody - LUL has performed alongside an awesome variety of bands in the Pacific Northwest. As a regional act, LUL aims to put on a stunning audio and visual performance to keep crowds coming back.
